Best Resume Builder for Free

These resume builder software options are completely free or have meaningful free tiers. I tested them all during my own job search.

HiringMessage - LaTeX Resume Builder (Free)
This is what I use. It's a LaTeX-based resume builder but you don't need to know LaTeX - just fill in your info and it generates a clean, ATS-friendly PDF. No buggy formatting, no fighting with margins. Built this after getting frustrated with every other resume builder software. Completely free.
Reactive Resume - Open Source Resume Builder
Open-source resume builder with real-time preview. Solid option if you want something quick and don't mind tweaking settings. Free and privacy-focused. Good alternative to paid resume builder software.
FlowCV - Simple Resume Builder
Has some nice templates. I tried this for non-engineering applications. The free tier is limited but enough if you just need one resume fast.
Standard Resume - Minimal Resume Builder
Very minimal, text-only designs. A friend in finance swears by this. If you're applying to consulting or law firms, this is probably better than flashier resume builder software.
JSON Resume - Developer Resume Builder
You write your resume in JSON format. Only use this if you're a developer who wants to version control your resume in git. Otherwise it's overkill.

ATS Checker & ATS Simulator Tools

ATS (Applicant Tracking System) software auto-rejects most resumes due to formatting issues. These ATS checker tools scan your resume before you apply.

Jobscan - ATS Simulator
Compares your resume to specific job postings and gives a match score. Acts as an ATS simulator to show what automated systems see. Limited free scans but useful for optimizing per application.
HiringMessage ATS Checker (Free)
Scans against 100+ rules validated with hiring managers from tech companies. I built this after my resume kept getting auto-rejected even though it looked fine. If you're in tech, this ATS checker catches issues others miss. Free.
Resume Worded - ATS Checker
Instant feedback on content and formatting. Free tier is limited but good for quick ATS checks before submitting.
TopResume - Human Review
One free review from an actual recruiter. Worth doing once to catch obvious issues you're blind to. Not technically an ATS simulator but helpful for real feedback.

AI Resume Builder & Cover Letter Tools

AI resume builder tools and cover letter generators can speed things up, but most sound robotic. Here's what actually works.

Cover Letter Copilot - AI Cover Letter
Generates cover letters from your resume + job posting. It's paid but the output is decent. Better than most free AI resume builder tools for cover letters.
Rezi - AI Resume Optimizer
Part of their whole suite. Matches keywords from job descriptions which helps with ATS. Functions as both AI resume builder and keyword optimizer. Freemium model.
Kickresume - Template-Based
More template-based than AI-generated. Some people prefer structure over letting AI resume builder tools write everything. Has free and paid tiers.

Job Boards

Wellfound (formerly AngelList)
Startup job board where you can message founders directly. Way better for early-stage companies than LinkedIn. Free.
Glassdoor
Use this to research companies before applying, not as your main job board. The salary data and reviews are more valuable than the postings.
Levels.fyi
Tech jobs with transparent comp data. If you're optimizing for money, start here.
Hacker News Who's Hiring
Monthly thread on HN. Completely free, direct contact with companies. Posts the first of every month. If you're a developer, check this.

Application Tracking

Huntr
Kanban board for job applications. I used this for about 3 months. The drag-and-drop is satisfying but honestly I ended up just using a spreadsheet.
Teal
Job tracker + resume builder software + Chrome extension. Does a lot. Maybe too much? Freemium model.
Google Sheets
What I use now. Columns: Company, Role, Date Applied, Status, Follow-up Date, Notes. Simple and I can access it anywhere.

Interview Prep

Pramp
Free peer mock interviews. You interview someone, they interview you. Great for technical and behavioral prep. Helped me way more than doing LeetCode alone.
LeetCode
2000+ coding problems. Free tier is enough. I did about 100 problems before interviews. Hated every minute but it worked.
Glassdoor Interview Questions
Real questions from specific companies. Check this before any interview - sometimes you get the exact questions they ask.
STAR Method
Not a tool, just a framework. Situation, Task, Action, Result. Structure your behavioral answers this way. Google it, practice it, use it.

LinkedIn Optimization

Shield
Chrome extension that shows who viewed your profile. Free. Useful for seeing if recruiters actually looked at you after applying.
Headline Tip
Your LinkedIn headline should not be "Student at Carleton University." Make it "Systems Engineering Student | Building SaaS Tools" or "Engineering Student | Interested in [specific field]." Recruiters search by keywords, not by school.
PhotoFeeler
Get your LinkedIn photo rated. Free tier available. Your photo matters more than you think - I changed mine after rating poorly and got more profile views immediately.

FAQ: Resume Builder Software & ATS Questions

What is the best resume builder for free?
The best free resume builder depends on your needs. HiringMessage is a LaTeX-based resume builder that's completely free and generates clean, ATS-friendly PDFs without requiring coding knowledge. Reactive Resume is another solid open-source option. For developers, JSON Resume offers version control for your resume in git. Avoid resume builder software that requires payment to download your PDF.
What is an ATS checker and do I need one?
An ATS (Applicant Tracking System) checker scans your resume to see if it will pass automated resume screening software that employers use. Most resumes get auto-rejected due to formatting issues, not qualifications. Tools like HiringMessage ATS checker scan against 100+ rules validated with hiring managers. If you're getting auto-rejected, an ATS simulator can help identify formatting problems before you apply.
Are AI resume builders worth it?
AI resume builder tools can speed up the process, but most generate generic content that sounds robotic. They're best used for formatting and structure, not writing. LaTeX-based resume builder software like HiringMessage focuses on clean formatting rather than AI content generation, which tends to work better for technical roles. Use AI resume builder tools for keyword optimization, not for writing your entire resume.
What is an ATS simulator?
An ATS simulator tests how your resume performs in Applicant Tracking Systems used by employers. It checks formatting, keyword matching, and parsing accuracy. Tools like Jobscan and HiringMessage act as ATS simulators, showing you exactly what automated systems see when they scan your resume. Using an ATS simulator before applying can prevent auto-rejection.
Can I use resume builder software for free?
Yes, several resume builder software options are completely free. HiringMessage, Reactive Resume, and JSON Resume are all free forever. FlowCV and Standard Resume have limited free tiers. Avoid resume builders that require payment to download your PDF - that's a common trap. The best resume builder for free should let you export PDFs without upgrading.
How do I know if my resume is ATS-friendly?
Use an ATS checker to scan your resume before applying. Common ATS issues include: fancy formatting, tables, headers/footers, images, and unusual fonts. LaTeX-based resume builders typically generate ATS-friendly PDFs automatically. Test with tools like HiringMessage or Jobscan (both act as ATS simulators) to verify your resume will parse correctly in Applicant Tracking Systems.
